Tabletop Radio

Sat 20th and Sun 21st June, 12pm - 1am Both Days

What is it?

Charity fundraising has suffered greatly due to the COVID-19 pandemic, and our usual in-person charity fundraising during our annual Tabletop Society Pride Week can no longer happen. So, as part of our Tabletop Pride Week (15th-21st June, Term 3 Week 9), we will be participating in the 2.6 challenge.

We wanted something to bring together our community in this isolated time, and we wanted something that echoed the marathon aspect of our Tabletop Weekends. And so we settled on an epic 26 hour charity stream (bold, I know) to tie into the "2.6" theme, with a lot of segments incorporating activities that we and the people in our community love.

We're raising money for Stonewall, an LGBT-focused charity that is committed to empowering LGBT individuals, transforming the culture of institutions towards inclusivity and acceptance, changing hearts and minds to value and respect LGBT individuals, and changing and protecting laws to contribute to LGBT equality.

Stream Sponsorship

Warwick Tabletop Games and Roleplaying Society has a membership of more than 230 people who are super into tabletop gaming of many varieties, including wargames, board games, card games (including Magic: the Gathering and Netrunner), RPGs (including D&D), and LARP.

We can bring you, as an individual or company, to the attention of this audience if you sponsor our stream via a donation to our JustGiving page. You will get an ad spot in our stream interludes, a logo and/or link on this webpage, and a shout out in the next edition of the weekly Tabletop Society newsletter.

How to apply

Are you an individual or company that wants a space in our ad breaks? Then follow these instructions:

  1. Send us an email at su303@warwicksu.com with TABLETOP RADIO SPONSOR as the subject line. Please include who you are/what your business is and the kind of ad you want us to show for you (a static image, prerecorded video, or text for an ad read).
  2. We will respond as to whether we’re happy to advertise you. Tabletop-related ads are prioritised. Whether your ad is used is at our discretion; we reserve the right to turn you down if we do not think your ad is appropriate for our stream.
  3. You send us your ad for approval!
  4. When approved, you should make the relevant donation to our JustGiving page.
  5. We will then display your ad during our stream interludes.
